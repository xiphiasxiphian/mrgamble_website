Mr Gamble - it's been a hell of a journey. You came in during Year 11 and completely transformed the subject for me, and made me fall back in love with it. Without your expert knowledge, passion and delivery, I firmly believe I would not be where I am today, not only within Computer Science, but as a person as a whole. I would like to also take this opportunity to thank you for all the help you gave me with my NEA because without it J would have been pretty stuck.

We've had our ups and downs along the way of course (mainly over coding), so I will apologise for that one final time, but on the whole I will look back on it with great fondness. I will certainly miss the Friday morning snacks, the tangents we go off on, the hidden references in the powerpoints and the coding projects you always managed to find to bring the course to life.

Computer Science was hands down my favourite subject at school, and you were a huge part of that - I feel so lucky to have had the privilege of being taught by you. So thank you Mr Gamble; thank you for everything. I wish you all the best in the future (even though I will never forget that 5%).

Robson
